Hari Ananta Hari Katha Ananta

That is The Lord is boundless, His Stories are boundless. When the Lord, the Parama PurusÌa1 decides to take the human form, from the moment He is born until the moment He leaves the mortal body, with every second, every breath He achieves His sankalp2 and sets His leela 3. This website is for devotees who get inspired, cherish, and drown in devotion listening to His leela.

At the time of Lord Shiva, scripts were not invented yet 4, but shrutii (spoken) languages were prevalent. Hence a large portion of Lord Shiva’s stories were eventually lost. During Lord Krishna’s time, script was already invented. Hence much of His life, events, His leelas were documented. This time we hope to do better. To help with that we want to document all experiences, along with references, chronology, and all possible details of Shrii Shrii Anandamurtii, our beloved Baba. Structure of website is as follows:

  • List of articles.
  • Catalogue.
  • Other resources.
  • Get in touch
